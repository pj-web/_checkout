The most important question in the market right now
“In January, bonds were still in a technical bull market as defined by the broad declining channel that had contained the 40 year bull market. In March the break of that downtrend turned the macro trend from bullish to neutral. The decline begs the question: Is the decline the result of the decades long negative correlation between equity and fixed income reasserting itself on the back of equity weakness? Or is it simply the beginning of a relief rally created by the combination of major support and a deeply oversold condition?”
